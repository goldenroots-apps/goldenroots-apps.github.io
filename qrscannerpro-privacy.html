Privacy Policy – QR Scanner Pro+

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Overview

QR Scanner Pro+ & Barcode (by Golden Roots) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Camera Access

The app uses your device's camera to scan QR codes and barcodes. All scan processing is done locally on your device. No camera data is transmitted or stored externally.

Photo Library Access

The app may access your photo library to scan QR codes from saved images. Selected images are processed locally and are not uploaded to any server.

Scan History

Your scan history, including scanned content and optional thumbnail images, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via Google Drive.

Google Drive Backup

QR Scanner Pro+ offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. When you choose to use this feature:

  • The app requests access to Google Drive's app-specific storage (appDataFolder) only.
  • Your scan history data is encrypted in transit and stored in a private folder that only this app can access.
  • No other files on your Google Drive are accessed, read, or modified.
  • You can disconnect your Google account or delete the backup at any time through the app settings.

Google Sign-In

If you use the Google Drive backup feature, you will be asked to sign in with your Google account. The app only accesses your basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) to display which account is connected. This information is not stored on any external server.

Advertising

The app displays ads using the following advertising SDKs:

  • Google AdMob: May collect device identifiers, app interactions, and diagnostics data for ad serving and personalization, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • AppLovin: May collect device identifiers, app interactions, and diagnostics data for ad mediation and personalization, in accordance with AppLovin's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as app opens, feature usage, crash logs, and diagnostics. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data Collected by Third-Party SDKs

The following data may be automatically collected by third-party SDKs integrated in the app:

  • Device or other IDs: Advertising ID and device identifiers (collected by AppLovin, AdMob)
  • App interactions: App usage data, feature interactions (collected by Firebase Analytics, AppLovin, AdMob)
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, performance data (collected by Firebase Analytics)

This data is collected automatically and is used for advertising, analytics, and app improvement purposes. It is encrypted in transit.

No Account Required

The app is fully functional without creating an account or providing any personal information. Google Sign-In is only required for the optional Google Drive backup feature.

Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their data as follows:

1. In-App Scan History

Go to the History tab → long press or swipe to delete individual items, or use "Delete All" to clear all scan history, favorites, and saved images.

2. Google Drive Backup Data

Go to Settings → tap your connected Google account → tap "Disconnect" to revoke access. Then go to Google Drive → Settings → Manage Apps → find "QR Scanner Pro+" and select "Delete hidden app data" to permanently remove backup files.

3. All Local Data

Uninstall the app from your device to remove all locally stored data including scan history, favorites, images, and app settings.

Data Types Affected

  • Deleted: Scan history, saved QR/barcode data, captured images, favorites, and Google Drive backup files.
  • Not stored on our servers: We do not maintain any user data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive.
  • Third-party data: Data collected by third-party SDKs (AppLovin, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics) is subject to their respective privacy policies and retention schedules.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
