About Us

Our Story

Golden Roots is a small, passionate team dedicated to creating mobile applications that simplify everyday tasks. Founded with the belief that technology should be accessible, intuitive, and respectful of user privacy.

We started with a simple idea: build apps that we ourselves would love to use — fast, reliable, and free from unnecessary complexity.

Our Mission

To craft simple, powerful mobile applications that save people time and make daily tasks effortless. We believe great software should work seamlessly, respect your privacy, and be available to everyone.

Our Values

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. We design our apps so your information stays on your device. No tracking, no selling data, no external servers storing your personal information.

Simplicity

We believe the best apps are the ones that just work. No bloated features, no confusing interfaces — just clean, focused tools that do what you need.

Quality

Every feature is carefully tested and optimized for performance. We'd rather do fewer things exceptionally well than many things poorly.

Accessibility

Our apps support multiple languages and are designed to be used by everyone, regardless of technical expertise. Technology should break down barriers, not create them.

Our Apps

We build tools that are fast, private, and easy to use.

QR Scanner Pro+ & Barcode

Our flagship app — a fast, reliable QR code and barcode scanner with scan history, favorites, optional Google Drive backup, and beautiful themes. Available on Google Play with 12,000+ downloads.

More apps are coming soon. We're always working on new tools to make your daily life simpler.
