Privacy Policy – QR Scanner Pro+

Last updated: 30 May 2025

QR Scanner Pro+ does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app uses your device’s camera only to scan QR codes and barcodes. All scan data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally.

The app may display ads using Google AdMob. These ads may collect anonymous usage data for ad personalization, in accordance with Google’s advertising policies.

We do not require users to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information. The app is fully functional without any form of registration.
