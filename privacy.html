Privacy Policy – QR Scanner Pro+

Last updated: 29 March 2026

Overview

QR Scanner Pro+ respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Camera Access

The app uses your device's camera to scan QR codes and barcodes. All scan processing is done locally on your device. No camera data is transmitted or stored externally.

Photo Library Access

The app may access your photo library to scan QR codes from saved images. Selected images are processed locally and are not uploaded to any server.

Scan History

Your scan history, including scanned content and optional thumbnail images, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via Google Drive.

Google Drive Backup

QR Scanner Pro+ offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. When you choose to use this feature:

  • The app requests access to Google Drive's app-specific storage (appDataFolder) only.
  • Your scan history data is encrypted in transit and stored in a private folder that only this app can access.
  • No other files on your Google Drive are accessed, read, or modified.
  • You can disconnect your Google account or delete the backup at any time through the app settings.

Google Sign-In

If you use the Google Drive backup feature, you will be asked to sign in with your Google account. The app only accesses your basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) to display which account is connected. This information is not stored on any external server.

Advertising

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data and device identifiers for ad personalization, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings.

Analytics

The app may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as app opens and feature usage. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

No Account Required

The app is fully functional without creating an account or providing any personal information. Google Sign-In is only required for the optional Google Drive backup feature.

Data Retention & Deletion

All scan history is stored locally on your device. You can delete your scan history at any time from within the app. If you use Google Drive backup, you can disconnect your account to stop syncing and delete the backup data from the app settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
