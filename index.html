QR Scanner Pro+

QR Scanner Pro+ is a fast and reliable QR code and barcode scanner app for Android. Scan codes using your camera, photo library, or URL. Back up your scan history securely to Google Drive.

Features

  • Scan QR codes and barcodes instantly with your camera
  • Scan from saved images or URLs
  • Save scan history with thumbnails
  • Backup and restore via Google Drive
  • Multiple color themes
  • App lock with PIN and biometric
